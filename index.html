Data Science and Analytics Projects

12 months sales forecast for a B2B company.

I used the historical data to forecast how sales numbers were expected to be in a 12 months data frame, if the company would keep its operations as it is. I analyzed data from organic and paid marketing channels to see which one would bring more leads and better conversion rates and should have more investments.
This analysis enables the company to understand how the company is growing and take actions with advanced time, in case the predictions are bellow the expectations.

Tools used for this project

  • Python, Pandas, Datetime.
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • More

RFM Analysis for marketing targeting strategies.

Using Google Sheets and its functions, I did a customer segmentation to divide the company’s clients in 6 clusters: High Value, Core, New, Promising, Need Attention and, Lost.
The result of this analysis allows marketers target specifics groups of existing customers with communications and offers that are more likely to be relevant for them. This leads to improvement in customer life time value (LTV), customer retention, customer satisfaction and, increase in response rates. That way, the company will save in marketing and increase its revenue.
